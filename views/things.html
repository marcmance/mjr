Things to do
Listed below are points of interests relative to the Radisson where you can spend time before, after or in between the ceremony and reception. You can shop, play, eat or drink at these suggested nearby attractions.
Freehold Mall
Shopping mall and movie theater just a mile away.
iPlay America
Huge indoor fun center 2 miles away with bumper cars, go-karts, laser tag and video games, plus a bar and grill, making it fun for all ages.
Downtown Freehold
The historic Downtown Freehold is full of resturants, shops, cafes and bars only 2 miles away.
Asbury Park / Belmar / Ocean Grove
These are the closest beaches and boardwalks, roughly 15-20 miles away (a short half hour drive).
